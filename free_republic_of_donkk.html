The Free Republic of Donkk (FRD) is a faction server within the game Stormworks: Build and Rescue based on the island of Donkk. They are a thriving server built on the values of democracy and freedom. They participate in and/or host events involving battles, rallies, and more.


Quote and description from the President of the FRD, Bjorn:

"When the short-lived Republic of Donkk molted it skin, a freer form of itself was birthed. For the first time in one hundred twenty-two years, the workers, farmers, miners, wives, and soldiers of the island of Donkk could rest on their laurels, and place their liberties in a newfound cradle of freedom."

The Free Republic of Donkk, abbreviated "F.R.D.," is a Presidential Republic founded July 22nd, 2024. It is complete with an armed Navy, Air Force, Army and Coastguard.

Trainings, internals, battles, raids, and more are frequently hosted within our ranks. We offer a challenging and entertaining promotion system, complete with many branch-exclusive ranks to rise through.

Like all other nations in the community, the F.R.D. only uses vehicles made by its own members. If you believe you have a vehicle worth submitting, promotions are available.

F.R.D. gives all of its members the opportunity to create a company to work under. Whether it be building weapons or hosting your own events, we encourage everyone to give it a try.
