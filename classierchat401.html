ClassierChat401 is a discord server that was founded on may 27th, 2023. It was initaly supposed to be a small scale friend server, but it evolved into one of the places people came to during the collapse of CGA and it is now home to out main friend sphere. It has now developed back into mainly a friend-sphere. The server is not based around a single game, but multiple different ones, such as Stormworks and Helldivers just to name a couple.
